Welcome to Florentinos!

Family Owned and Operated since 1990

At Florentino's Catering, our mission is to create unforgettable culinary experiences by combining exceptional flavors, fresh ingredients, and impeccable service. We are committed to exceeding our clients' expectations, whether for an intimate gathering or a grand celebration. Since 1990, we strive to bring people together through delicious food, creativity, and personalized service. Our goal is to make every event special, ensuring that every bite tells a story of quality, tradition, and innovation.

Meet the Owners:

Dave Yasser and Lenny DePinto have owned Florentinos since 1990. They have been a family owned and operated business dedicated to providing handcrafted food and exceptional service at affordable prices, all while actively giving back to the Monmouth County community. Dave and Lenny, alongside their dedicated team of employees, work together to uphold the highest standards of quality and service, creating a welcoming and exceptional experience for every customer. Their passion for high-quality ingredients and attention to detail has earned Florentino's a reputation as a local favorite. Whether you're stopping in for a quick bite or catering a special event, their commitment to excellence ensures that every customer leaves satisfied.
